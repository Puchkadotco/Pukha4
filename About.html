Our Story

Street puchka has always been a favorite snack, but hygiene has often been a concern. We started Brand Name with one mission—to serve the same authentic street taste in the cleanest possible way. Today, our modern cart, fresh ingredients, purified water, and unique six-flavour experience allow everyone to enjoy puchka with complete confidence. Every crunchy bite reminds us why we started this journey—to spread happiness through great taste.

🎯 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted hygienic street-food brand while preserving the authentic taste everyone loves.
