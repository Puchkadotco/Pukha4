Welcome to Puchka.Co

At Puchka.Co, we believe everyone deserves delicious street food without worrying about hygiene. Our specially designed puchka cart combines authentic street flavors with modern cleanliness. Every crispy puchka is filled with freshly prepared potato stuffing, premium spices, and served with six signature flavored waters made using purified drinking water. Every bite is crunchy, spicy, tangy, refreshing, and guaranteed to leave you craving more.

Our Story

Puchka.Co has always been a favorite snack, but hygiene has often been a concern. We started Puchka.Co with one mission—to serve the same authentic street taste in the cleanest possible way. Today, our modern cart, fresh ingredients, purified water, and unique six-flavour experience allow everyone to enjoy puchka with complete confidence. Every crunchy bite reminds us why we started this journey—to spread happiness through great taste.

🎯 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted hygienic street-food brand while preserving the authentic taste everyone loves.
