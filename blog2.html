Our 6 Signature Water Flavours

Six Unique Flavours. One Unforgettable Experience.

At Puchka.Co, we believe that the soul of every delicious puchka lies in its flavoured water. While the crispy shell and fresh filling are important, it is the burst of tangy, spicy, sweet, and refreshing water that creates the unforgettable experience our customers love. That's why we carefully crafted six signature flavours that suit every taste preference.

Made Fresh Every Day

Every morning our team prepares fresh flavoured water using purified drinking water, mint leaves, coriander, roasted spices, tamarind, lemons, green chillies, garlic, and carefully selected ingredients. We never compromise on freshness because authentic taste begins with quality ingredients.

"Every flavour tells a different story, but every story begins with freshness."

Our Six Signature Flavours

  • 🌿 Mint Magic – Cool, refreshing and packed with fresh mint leaves for the classic street-food experience.
  • 🥭 Raw Mango Blast – Tangy raw mango combined with aromatic spices for a refreshing twist.
  • 🍋 Lemon Twist – Fresh lemon juice blended with traditional Indian spices to create a light citrus flavour.
  • 🧄 Garlic Blast – A bold and spicy flavour made especially for customers who enjoy rich garlic notes.
  • 🍬 Sweet Tamarind Delight – A perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess prepared from premium tamarind.
  • 🌶️ Fiery Masala Water – Our hottest signature flavour created for true spice lovers.

Something for Everyone

Whether you enjoy sweet flavours, spicy flavours, tangy flavours, or refreshing flavours, our menu has something you'll love. Many customers even enjoy mixing different flavours to create their own unique Puchka.Co experience.

Consistency You Can Trust

Every batch follows our carefully tested recipes, ensuring that the flavour you enjoy today will taste just as amazing tomorrow. Consistency is one of the reasons why customers continue to return to Puchka.Co.

Experience the Difference

Our six signature waters are more than just flavours—they are part of our promise to deliver authentic Indian street food with premium hygiene and unforgettable taste. Every sip adds excitement to every bite, making every visit to Puchka.Co a memorable experience.
